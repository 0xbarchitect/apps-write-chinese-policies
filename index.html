1) Information We Collect

A. Information you provide

  • Google Sign-In (optional): If you sign in with Google, we may receive basic profile information such as your Google account identifier, display name, and email address (depending on what Google provides and what you consent to).
  • Support communications: If you contact us, you may provide information like your email address and the content of your message.

B. Learning progress and app content

The App teaches Chinese writing lessons and tracks learning progress. We may collect and store:

  • Progress data such as lessons completed, star ratings/score, practice counts, and time spent.
  • Leaderboard-related data such as your display name (or a nickname), rank, stars, and time accomplishments if you participate in ranking features.

C. Usage, device, and diagnostic information

Like most apps, we and/or our service providers may collect certain technical data to run the App, measure performance, and fix bugs.

  • Device & app info: device model, OS version, app version, language, time zone, and similar technical details.
  • Diagnostics: crash logs, error reports, and performance data (e.g., app responsiveness). This may include the time a crash occurred, what screen was open, and basic device/app state at the time.
  • Identifiers: for ads and fraud prevention, third parties (e.g., AdMob) may use the device’s advertising identifier (such as Google Advertising ID) and related signals.

2) How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • Provide core features: deliver lessons, handwriting practice, daily usage limits, rewarded usage, and progress tracking.
  • Account features: enable Google sign-in, sync/restore progress (if implemented), and associate progress with your account.
  • Leaderboard: compute ranks and display your progress metrics on ranking boards.
  • Ads and purchases: show ads (including rewarded ads) and process in-app purchases.
  • Improve and secure the App: analytics, debugging, preventing abuse, and maintaining reliability.
  • Customer support: respond to your requests and troubleshoot issues.

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. If you choose to watch a rewarded ad, you can gain additional usage beyond the daily limit. Ad providers may collect or receive certain information (such as advertising identifiers and device signals) to show ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.
